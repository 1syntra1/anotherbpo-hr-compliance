About the GBS Incentive

The Global Business Services (GBS) incentive is administered by the Department of Trade, Industry and Competition (the dtic) to support offshore service delivery and job creation.

  • Employees must work 40+ hours/week (excl. lunch).
  • No job displacement of local roles.
  • Offshore activity must be proven via contracts & invoices.
  • Claims require external auditor sign-off.
