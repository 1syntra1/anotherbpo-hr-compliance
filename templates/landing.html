How It Works

From data to submission in 4 steps

The tool walks you through the full compliance cycle — capture once, submit everywhere.

1
Load Employee Data
Upload a CSV/Excel file or add employees manually. All EE, payroll, and COIDA fields in one place.
2
Configure Each Module
Enter company details, SETA, SDL number, COIDA classes, and BBBEE scorecard data per submission.
3
Review & Calculate
Matrices and scores are calculated live. Check your EE profile, BBBEE level, levy estimate before generating.
4
Generate & Submit
Download ready-to-submit Excel, PDF, or Word reports for each statutory body — DoEL, SETA, Comp Fund.
